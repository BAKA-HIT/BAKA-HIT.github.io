About

I am an incoming Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Nanjing University, advised by Prof. Shi Li. Currently, I am taking a gap period before starting my Ph.D. study. Previously, I received my M.Eng. and B.Eng. degrees from the Faculty of Computing at Harbin Institute of Technology, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Dongjing Miao. My research interests include approximation algorithms, combinatorial optimization, and theoretical aspects of data management and machine learning.
